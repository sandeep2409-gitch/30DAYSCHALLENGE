## 💡 Pro Tips for Engagement

1. **Always ask a question** to spark discussion
2. **Use 3-5 emojis** but don't overdo it
3. **Keep text scannable** - use bullets and line breaks
4. **Include a screenshot/GIF** of your app working
5. **Tag relevant people** (not spam, genuinely relevant)
6. **Respond to EVERY comment** in first 2 hours
7. **Post at 8-9 AM** your timezone for peak engagement
8. **Use hashtags strategically**: #30WebAppsIn30Days #BuildInPublic #FullStack
9. **Share your GitHub link** (easy to save/reference)
10. **Be authentic** - people connect with real struggle, not polish
